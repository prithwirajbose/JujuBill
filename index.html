JujuBill

Billing Application Suite by JujuApps.com

What is JujuBill

JujuBill is a Web Application with which you can manage Billing and Subscription of your customers. JujuBill has a simple and easy to manage Admin Interface. You can quickly manage your customers, configure their subscriptions and enjoy hassle free Billing Cycles. Your customer would be able to see Subscription details and pay the bills online using Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Paypal, Indian Bank Payment Gateway and many other options.

Why JujuBill

It's true that JujuBill is not the first one in its class, but very few of them are Open Source, Free and developed with scalable and cross platform compatible Java Technology.
